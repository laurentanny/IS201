Here is a small list of my hobbies! Just some things I love to do or find interesting.


  1. Running
    • I ran the St. George Marathon this year! Honestly, it was the hardest thing I've ever done, but I am already signed up to run my next marathon in August 2024 because I had major FOMO when I heard my friends were signing up.
    • I also try to run at least 4x per week just to stay in the groove. Sometimes, if you take a break from running, it is so hard to get back into it.
  2. Spending Time with my Friends!
    • My friends are a huge part of my life. I consider them my family!
    • When I'm not in class or running alone, I am typically with my friends.
  3. Tennis
    • I have played tennis since I was 6 years old! It is something I love to play in the Spring and Summer.
    • My favorite player is Maria Sharapova. She has been my idol since I was young and I got to see her play in the 2014 US Open.
  4. Painting
    • I have painted since I was a little girl. My parents put me in art classes because I didn't want to play an instrument like the rest of my siblings. It has been a lifelong hobby that I love!
    • This is my favorite painting: Carnation, Lily, Lily, Rose by John Singer Sargent
  5. Music
    • I listen to music while doing everything! It is a huge part of my life.
    • This was my most listened to song this year, so it felt appropriate to add a link to the music video!
    • Justin Timberlake - Mirrors
